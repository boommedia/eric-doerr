⚠️ Permits & Code — West Palm Beach

Don't skip the 10-minute phone check

Enclosing a patio + extending AC likely needs a zoning/building permit. Converting a garage to a bedroom may trigger an occupancy permit, and it needs a legal second egress + light/ventilation to count as a bedroom. Electrical work may require inspection.

📞 Palm Beach County Building Dept: 561-355-2990 — call for a pre-check before buying materials. Budget $100–$300 for permits (adjust the line item above).
