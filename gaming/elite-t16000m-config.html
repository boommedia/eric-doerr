🚀 Can't take off? When the HUD says "SHIP SECURED — HOLD [JOY Y-AXIS] TO LAUNCH", you must push/pull the stick to FULL deflection and HOLD for 2–3 seconds — tapping does nothing. Pull fully back and hold; if that fails, push fully forward and hold (one direction is "thrust up"). This is Landing Override: while landing gear is down, your pitch axis becomes vertical thrust.
🥾 Disembark = THROTTLE hat DOWN. It opens the Role Panel (bottom panel) — "Disembark" is a menu option in there, not a keybind, which is why you could never find it in Options. Note it is the throttle's hat, not the stick's (stick hat-down = reset pips). See your actual controls.

🧠 The one thing to understand

Elite has three completely separate binding sets — Ship, SRV (Scarab/Scorpion) and On Foot. A key bound in one does nothing in the others. Your Thrustmaster T16000M FCS HOTAS preset fills in the Ship set (and much of the SRV set) but leaves most On Foot actions blank — that's the gap you keep hitting. Fill the walk set with keyboard + mouse.

🛠️ Setting up the Walk (On Foot) set

  1. Options ▸ Controls — leave the preset on Thrustmaster T16000M FCS HOTAS (it keeps your flight binds).
  2. Scroll the middle list down past the interface categories to the ON FOOT category and expand it with +.
  3. Any row with an empty box is unbound. Click the box and press the key you want.
  4. Bind at minimum: Move F/B/L/R, Sprint, Jump, Crouch, Primary Fire, Aim, Reload, Interact, Melee, Grenade, Tool wheel.
  5. If a key flags a red conflict, pick a different one — on-foot conflicts with ship binds are fine to ignore only if they're in different sets.
  6. Back out (it auto-saves) and test on foot.
⚠️ Don't switch the preset to a keyboard one to "fix" walking — that wipes your HOTAS flight binds. Add the on-foot keys on top of the T16000M preset.

Can't launch from a padHold the launch axis at full deflection 2–3s (not a tap). Check the HUD prompt for the exact control.
Can't disembark / no "Disembark"Use the control the HUD names — on this preset POV hat DOWN. Must be stationary. Requires Odyssey.
Stuck in the SRVRole panel → Self-Destruct, or Recall Ship and board it. Works with no binds at all.
