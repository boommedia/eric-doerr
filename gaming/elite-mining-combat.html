Step-by-step
  1. Find a hotspot: honk the system, fire a DSS probe at a ring to reveal hotspots (overlaps = richer), or look one up on Inara.
  2. Drop into the ring and switch on the Pulse Wave Analyser — good rocks glow.
  3. Prospect a rock with a limpet to confirm contents/%.
  4. Mine: laser deposits, or for cores drop a Seismic Charge, let it crack, then Abrasion Blaster the chunks.
  5. Scoop & refine with collector limpets + refinery.
  6. Sell where demand is high (Inara shows best buyers).

Step-by-step (first FPS run)
  1. At a station Concourse, buy a Dominator suit + a laser and a kinetic weapon from Pioneer Supplies (or grab a pre-fit at a CZ).
  2. Pick a fight: Frontline Solutions for a Conflict Zone, or take settlement missions from the board.
  3. Get there via an Apex shuttle or fly your own ship & drop an SRV / land nearby.
  4. Fight smart: use cover, strip shields with laser/plasma, finish with kinetic, aim for headshots; recharge with energy cells.
  5. Loot bodies & containers (E-Breach locked ones); complete the objective & extract.
  6. Cash in: sell loot to the bartender, exobio to Vista Genomics; upgrade suits/weapons at the bartender using collected materials.
⚠️ Watch your battery & oxygen — the suit powers sprint, shields, night-vision & tools; O2 is limited on airless worlds. Refill at consumable points or your ship.

Core loop

🎙️ Go hands-free with VoiceAttack

VoiceAttack lets you control Elite with your voice — it listens for a spoken phrase and presses the key you'd normally press. Pair it with a voice pack (e.g. HCS) for a talking AI co-pilot. Perfect for keeping both hands on the T16000M while you fire off pip presets, deploy limpets or request docking.
Set it up
  1. Install VoiceAttack (~$10) from voiceattack.com and set up your Windows microphone.
  2. (Optional) add a voice pack — an HCS pack gives a co-pilot personality plus hundreds of ready-made Elite commands.
  3. Import a profile — voice packs & community profiles ship a .vap Elite command set; import it in VoiceAttack.
  4. Match the keybinds — VoiceAttack just presses keys, so every command's target key must be bound in Elite (and on your HOTAS).
  5. Pick push-to-talk or always-listening, then test a command and watch it fire.

⛏️ Mining runs
💎 The Opal RushEasy–Med
🚀 Type-11 Prospector / Python · 💰 big single-loop payout

Core-mine Void Opals & Low Temp. Diamonds in an icy hotspot, fill the hold, cash out in one loop.

  1. Find a Void Opal / LTD icy hotspot — classic anchor: Borann A 2.
  2. Fit Seismic Charge + Abrasion Blaster + Pulse Wave Analyser.
  3. Crack cores, blast & scoop chunks, refine until full.
  4. Sell to the highest-demand market within ~50 ly.

🧊 Diamond CruiseEasy
🚀 Type-11 Prospector · 💰 steady & low-stress

Chill laser-mining loop on Low Temp. Diamonds — no core cracking, just lasers, limpets & podcasts.

  1. Drop into an LTD icy hotspot, Pulse Wave Analyser on.
  2. Prospect glowing rocks, laser the surface deposits.
  3. Let collector limpets & the refinery do the work.
  4. Repeat until full, then sell.

🎯 Bounty-hunting runs
🚨 RES Warm-UpEasy
🚀 Python · 💰 safe, steady bounties

Learn the ropes at a Low/Standard RES where system security fights alongside you.

  1. Go to a ringed planet in a high-security system (e.g. Deciat) and drop at a Low RES.
  2. Scan ships; only engage Wanted ones — let cops soak damage.
  3. Lasers to drop shields, multi-cannons on hull.
  4. Cash bounties at a station's Contacts.

🚀 Anaconda / Python · 💰 top bounties, no cops

Solo the big money at a Hazardous RES — no security to help, so bring a tank build.

  1. Drop at a Hazardous RES in a ringed system.
  2. Pick off single targets, keep 4 pips to SYS.
  3. Use chaff/heat sinks; boost out if swarmed.
  4. Stack Kill Warrant Scanner bounties before cashing in.

🥾 Boots on the GroundMedium
🥽 Dominator suit · 💰 merc pay

Your first on-foot firefight — sign up for a ground Conflict Zone and get paid to fight.

  3. Use cover; strip shields (laser) then finish (kinetic); headshots.

🕵️ The Data HeistMedium
🥽 Maverick suit · 💰 data & loot

Infiltrate a settlement quietly — clone a profile, hack the doors, grab the data, slip out.

  1. Take a theft/collect mission targeting a settlement.
  2. Maverick suit: use the Profile Analyser to clone clearance.
  3. E-Breach locked doors/containers; grab data & components.
  4. Avoid alarms; extract before security & skimmers arrive.
