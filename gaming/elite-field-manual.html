🥾 On-foot — from zero to a completed mission

🌱 Start with the easiest job that exists: a Salvage / Restore mission at an abandoned or damaged settlement. Little or no security, no time limit, and it teaches you the whole loop safely. Do not start with a Conflict Zone.
Before you leave the station
The run, step by step
  1. Dock at a station with a Concourse and disembark.If there's no Concourse (no bar/shops), it's not an Odyssey port — try another station.
  2. Pioneer Supplies: buy a Dominator suit (combat) or Maverick (salvage), plus one laser and one kinetic weapon.Laser strips shields, kinetic kills. Carrying only one type is why fights feel impossible.
  3. Stock consumables — energy cells, medkits, grenades, and E-Breach if you're looting.Energy cells recharge your shield AND suit battery mid-fight. Carry several.
  4. Mission board → take a Salvage / Restore / Reactivation job.These point you at a settlement with a nav target, so you never hunt blindly.
  5. Fly there. Land on the settlement pad if your ship fits, otherwise land nearby or drop the SRV.Your Python fits most pads; the Anaconda often won't.
  6. Disembark — POV hat DOWN, while stationary.
  7. Scout before entering. Dark, unlit settlement = abandoned (safe). Lights, ships and patrols = occupied (will turn hostile if you trespass).
  8. Do the objective: loot containers/bodies, or for a Restore find the Power Centre, insert the Power Regulator, then Energylink power to each building.
  9. Loot everything on the way out — containers, lockers, bodies. E-Breach the locked ones.This is where suit/weapon upgrade materials come from.
  10. Extract to your ship/SRV, fly back, hand in the mission and sell loot to the Bartender.

⚠️ Don't rush into Conflict Zones. Do 3–4 salvage/restore runs first, upgrade your suit a grade or two at the Bartender, then try a Low-intensity CZ.

🎯 Bounty hunting — get paid without dying

🌱 Start at a LOW RES, not Hazardous. System security fights alongside you — let them tank while you deal damage. Haz RES has no police and will delete you.
Before you undock
The run, step by step
  1. Pick a ringed planet in a high-security system, supercruise to it and drop at a Resource Extraction Site (Low).
  2. Don't shoot yet. Watch for the police engaging a ship — that ship is Wanted.Only Wanted ships pay. Shooting anything else makes YOU the criminal.
  3. Target it and scan — the HUD will show WANTED in red. A Kill Warrant Scanner reveals extra bounties from other jurisdictions.
  4. Engage with the cops. Lasers until the shield ring collapses, then multi-cannons into the hull.
  5. Manage pips constantly: 4 to SYS when taking fire, shift to ENG to boost away, WEP while shooting.Pips are the single biggest survivability lever in ship combat.
  6. Disengage when your shields drop. Boost away, let them recharge, come back.A rebuy costs far more than the bounty you were chasing.
  7. Cash in at a station → Contacts. If you picked up fines, use Interstellar Factors.
Why it's going wrong
ProblemCause & fix
I keep getting destroyedYou're at a High/Haz RES, or fighting alone. Go Low RES, fight alongside police, and run when shields fail.
Now I have a bounty on MEYou shot a clean ship or the police. Pay it off at Interstellar Factors (they take wanted commanders).
My guns do nothingWrong type or no power. Thermal (lasers) → shields, kinetic (multi-cannons) → hull. Put pips in WEP or your guns stop firing.
I run dry mid-fightMulti-cannons use ammo — restock at any station. Lasers never run out but drain WEP.
Nothing to shoot hereYou dropped at the wrong signal. Look specifically for RESOURCE EXTRACTION SITE in the nav panel.
Enemies escapeThey high-wake out. Target their drives, or ignore them — plenty more spawn.
I overheat constantlyToo many beam lasers. Mix in pulse/multi-cannons and fit a heat sink.
💡 Best value trick: stack massacre missions from several factions at a station before you go — each kill then counts toward multiple missions at once, multiplying your payout for the same effort.

⛏️ Mining — filling the Type-11's hold

🌱 Learn on laser mining first (Platinum/Painite in a metallic ring). Core mining pays more but has more steps to get wrong.
Before you undock
The run, step by step
  1. Find a hotspot — EDTools or EDMining, entering your current system.Icy rings = Void Opals/LTDs (gems). Metallic = Platinum/Painite (metals).
  2. Fly in, honk, then DSS-probe the ring and fly to where hotspots overlap.
  3. Drop into the ring and switch to ANALYSIS MODE (HUD turns blue) and deploy hardpoints.Mining tools do nothing in Combat mode. This is the #1 mistake.
  4. Fire the Pulse Wave Analyser — good asteroids flash bright orange/yellow.
  5. Prospector limpet the promising rock to confirm what's in it and the %.
  6. Laser the coloured surface patches until they shatter into fragments.
  7. Collector limpets scoop them into the refinery, which bins them into tonnes.
  8. Repeat until full, then sell where demand is highest (check Inara).
Core mining (the big money) — extra steps
  1. Look for a rock with a rotating fissure glow under the Pulse Wave — that's a core.
  2. Seismic Charge Launcher: fire charges into the fissures. A meter fills — stop in the blue "optimal" zone, don't overcharge into red.
  3. Back off ~1 km and wait for it to crack open.
  4. Abrasion Blaster the glowing chunks off the surface; collectors scoop them.

🤖 Solo force-multiplier: 2–3 Collector Limpet Controllers + 1 Prospector turns you into a one-ship fleet. Add a Fighter Hangar with a hired NPC pilot if pirates keep interrupting.
