👍 A one-handed keypad (like the Redragon K585 DITI) is a competitive advantage: every key sits under your fingers & thumb, the mouse does all the aiming, and the extra macro keys give you binds a normal keyboard can't reach. The goal: no finger ever has to leave home row to do something important.

🖐️ Ergonomics & positioning

  1. Home row on WASD. Index on D/F, middle on W, ring on A, so Q E R F G C V are all one small movement away.
  2. Thumb owns Space + macro column. Put Jump and your most-spammed action (heal, plate, ping) on thumb keys — thumbs are fast and never leave.
  3. Use the wrist rest & angle the pad so your wrist is straight — less fatigue over long sessions.
  4. Mouse arm free. Low-to-mid DPI + big mousepad = full-arm aim; the keypad frees desk space for it.

🧩 Redragon software, remapping & macros

  1. Install the Redragon software for your model (from the official Redragon site / product page). It lets you remap any key, record macros, set RGB profiles, and save per-game profiles.
  2. Make a profile per game — Apex, Warzone, REDSEC each get their own key map & macros; switch profiles when you switch games.
  3. Program the M1–M6 macro keys for things a single key can't do: a quick-heal / plate-up sequence, a comms/ping combo, or an emote/inspect. Keep macros within game rules — single-action rebinds are always safe; automated fire is bannable.
  4. Set actuation & N-key rollover — mechanical switches register fast; N-key rollover means every simultaneous press lands (strafe + jump + shoot + reload at once).
💡 Modifier layers: pick one key as a shift/Fn (a spare thumb or macro key) so Fn + W/A/S/D etc. become a second set of binds — doubles your reachable actions without moving your hand.

🎯 Binding strategy for one hand

Move (WASD)Home row — never remap these

Most-used ability / heal / plateQ, E, or a thumb/macro key — must be reachable mid-strafe
Reload / interactR / F (index finger)
Grenade / ultimate / gadgetG / C / V / macro keys

Rarely-used (map, emote)Tab / far keys — fine if you stop moving to press them
🖱️ Offload to the mouse. Put Ping, heal, or a tactical on your mouse side buttons so your keypad hand keeps moving & shooting.
