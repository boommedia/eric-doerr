🥾 How to disembark (the real answer)

"Disembark" is not a keybind at all — that's why you could never find it in Options. It's a menu option inside the Role Panel.

  1. Be fully stopped (docked, landed, or SRV parked).
  2. Press the hat switch DOWN on your TWCS THROTTLE THROTTLE POV1-Down — this opens the Role Panel (the bottom panel).
  3. Choose Disembark from the options in that panel.

⚠️ Not the stick's hat — on the stick, hat-down is "Reset pips". It's the throttle's hat that opens the Role Panel.

🚀 How to launch from a pad

Launch is on your stick's Y-axis — with landing gear down, pitch becomes vertical thrust.

Push/pull the stick to FULL deflection and HOLD 2–3 seconds. Tapping does nothing. If one direction doesn't lift you, hold the other.

⚠️ Three real gaps worth fixing (everything else is fine):
  1. SRV Drive & Steer are unbound — that's why driving felt wrong. Bind them in Options ▸ Controls ▸ Driving to your stick X-axis (steer) and throttle axis (drive), or W/S + A/D.
  2. Frame Shift Drive is unbound — bind it to a stick or throttle button so you can supercruise/jump without hunting for a key.
  3. Recall / Dismiss Ship is unbound — bind it; it's your escape hatch when stranded in an SRV.

📥 Can I just import controls?

You don't need to — yours are already configured. But for reference: Elite stores presets as .binds files in
%LOCALAPPDATA%\Frontier Developments\Elite Dangerous\Options\Bindings

Drop a .binds file in there and it appears in the preset dropdown in-game. You already have several, including "Dual T16000m space legs" — but it fails to load with "Missing devices: T16000M", because it expects two T16000M sticks and only one is plugged in. If you want to use that dual-stick profile, connect the second stick first.
