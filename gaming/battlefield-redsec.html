💥 Battlefield REDSEC — Settings & Gameplay Guide

Battlefield 6's free battle-royale mode — settings, the 4 classes, teamplay, destruction & vehicles.

🎖️ The 4 classes — pick your role

Battlefield 6 brings back the classic 4-class system. A balanced squad (one of each) beats four of the same — coordinate your gadgets.
ClassJobSignature gadgets
AssaultFrontline attacker — take & hold groundGrenades & mobility; aggressive pushing
EngineerAnti-vehicle & repairRocket/AT launchers, repair tool — kill or fix vehicles
SupportSustain the squadRevive/heal & ammo resupply — keep teammates up & loaded
ReconIntel & long rangeSpotting, sniper rifles, spawn beacons — reveal & reposition the squad
🩹 Support is king in a BR — reviving & re-arming your squad keeps you in the fight far longer. Have at least one Support and one Engineer (for vehicle threats) per squad.

📈 Play the Battlefield way

  1. Stick with your squad — spawn on teammates/beacons, push together, trade revives. Lone-wolves get mowed down.
  2. Use destruction — Battlefield lets you blow open walls & drop cover. Make your own angles; don't trust a wall to protect you.
  3. Spot enemies (Recon/ping) so the whole squad sees them — info wins.
  4. Respect vehicles — keep an Engineer with AT ready; don't cross open ground when armour is near.
  5. Loot & rotate — gear up, then move toward the safe zone using cover & buildings.
  6. Suppress & flank — pin enemies with fire while a squadmate takes an angle.

🔫 Weapons & customization

  1. Pick a versatile automatic you can control at range as your main.
  2. Use the gunsmith/customization to cut recoil & improve handling (barrel, grip, optic, mag).
  3. Match your optic to your role — red-dot/holo for close, low-mag for mid, scope for Recon.
  4. Carry a sidearm for emergencies and the right gadget for your class.
