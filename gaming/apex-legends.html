📈 Get better — fundamentals

  1. Warm up in the Firing Range — practice tracking a moving dummy & recoil control for your 2 main guns before you queue.
  2. Strafe while shooting — move left/right to dodge while keeping your crosshair on target.
  3. Movement tech: slide-jump down slopes for speed, bunny-hop while healing, tap-strafe (mouse) to change direction mid-air, and use ziplines/redeploys to reposition.
  4. Positioning > aim: take high ground, avoid open fields, and rotate early toward the next circle to hold strong spots.
  5. Beware the third party — after a fight, heal & move; other squads hear you and push.
  6. Play as a trio: ping everything, stick together, share loot & batteries.
