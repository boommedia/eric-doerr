🎒 Loadouts & "characters"

In Warzone, operators are cosmetic — they don't have abilities. Your real "build" is your Loadout: two weapons, perks & equipment. Buy loadouts at Buy Stations or find drops.

🔧 Gunsmith: most weapons feel meta once tuned — prioritise attachments that lower recoil & improve handling (barrel, stock, grip, mag, optic). Check a tier-list site for the current best guns, then copy the recommended build.

📈 Master the BR loop

  1. Land with a plan — drop near a Buy Station & loot fast; grab cash for your loadout + UAV.
  2. Plate up constantly — never fight below full armour; carry plates.
  3. Do a Contract (Bounty/Scavenger) early for cash, gear & intel.
  4. Win the Gulag — it's a 1v1 to respawn; if you die, this gets you back in.
  5. Buy back teammates at a Buy Station and grab a UAV before rotating.
  6. Rotate early & use cover — move toward the circle ahead of the gas, hold high ground/buildings, don't run in the open.
  7. Communicate: ping enemies, call rotations, third-party fights you hear.
