🧭 How to find your home (the workflow)

  1. Pick a faction you like above (Free Space Rangers is your strongest). Open its Inara page → note its controlled systems.
  2. Filter for ringed systems. On the EDTools hotspot finder, search near one of those systems — icy rings = Void Opals/LTDs, metallic = Painite/Platinum.
  3. Check for a RES in the same or a neighbouring system on the EDTools RES finder — that's your bounty hunting on the doorstep.
  4. Confirm the station has a Concourse (Odyssey services) and an Interstellar Factors contact — check the station on Inara.
  5. Score it below. The winner is the system that ticks the most boxes with the shortest hop between mining and bounties.
⚠️ You're currently deep in Col 285 Sector, far from the populated bubble. Faction-controlled systems with stations are mostly back in the bubble — a home base means committing to a region. If you want to stay out here, home = a Fleet Carrier instead (see Road to 5 Billion).
