🔧 What to load — mining fit

A hybrid core + laser miner: cores for the big Void Opal / LTD payouts, lasers for steady surface deposits. Tick each as you buy it in Outfitting — it saves in your browser.
⚠️ Load limpets! Buy a stack of Limpets from the station's Restock/Cargo before you leave — they're consumed as you mine, and no limpets = no auto-scooping.

🗺️ Where to go & make money

  1. Find the nearest hotspot. Open the EDTools hotspot finder, enter your current system (e.g. Pacheco), and sort for Void Opals or Low Temp. Diamonds in icy rings (best money), or Painite in metallic rings.
  2. Map the ring. Jump in, honk, fire a DSS probe at the ring, and fly to where the hotspots overlap (richer spawns).
  3. Mine. Pulse Wave Analyser on → prospect glowing rocks → crack cores (Seismic + Abrasion) or laser deposits → collector limpets scoop → refinery fills the hold.
  4. Sell high. Check the best sell price on Inara (or EDTools) for your material within ~50 ly and haul it there.
💎 Classic anchor: Borann A 2 has long been a famous Void Opal / LTD icy-ring mining spot — a solid destination if nothing closer shows up. Always confirm current prices & hotspots on the tools above (they shift).

🕹️ On your T16000M: bind Analysis/Combat mode toggle, next fire group, and primary/secondary fire to the stick trigger, thumb button & hat so you can cycle tools without the keyboard. Collector limpets auto-launch when fragments are nearby (with a controller fitted).
2 · Laser mining (steady income)
  1. Enter the ring, switch to Analysis Mode (blue HUD) and deploy hardpoints.
  2. Fire the Pulse Wave Analyser — asteroids flash; the bright/orange ones have the good stuff.
  3. Fly to a promising rock and fire a Prospector Limpet at it — it reports the contents & %. Look for your target material at a healthy percentage.
  4. Switch to Mining Lasers (Group A) and hold fire on the coloured surface deposits until they shatter into fragments.
  5. Collector limpets auto-scoop the fragments into the Refinery, which bins them into 1-tonne units.
  6. Move to the next rock and repeat until the hold is full.
3 · Core mining (the big Void Opal / LTD money)
  1. Pulse Wave Analyser on → look for a rock with a bright, rotating fissure glow — that's a core.
  2. Prospect it to confirm the material (Void Opals, LTDs, Alexandrite…).
  3. Switch to the Seismic Charge Launcher (Group B). Fire charges into the glowing fissures — a meter fills; stop in the blue "optimal" zone (don't overcharge into red).
  4. Back off ~1 km and wait — the asteroid cracks open and flings out chunks.
  5. Switch to the Abrasion Blaster and shoot the glowing deposits stuck to the surface & chunks to knock them loose.
  6. Grab any sub-surface deposits with the Displacement Missile (fire, then detonate at the right depth on the meter).
  7. Collector limpets scoop everything; the refinery fills. One good core can be worth tens of millions.
⚠️ Common gotchas: tools do nothing in Combat Mode — stay in Analysis (blue). Out of limpets = no auto-scoop. If the refinery hopper is full of low-value bits, vent them so good material has a bin.
