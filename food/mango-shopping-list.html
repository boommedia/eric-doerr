🛒 Amazon Shopping Tips

  • Spice Bundles: Search "spice set" or "spice rack" to get multiple spices at a discount
  • Bulk Produce: Order through Amazon Fresh or Prime Now if available in your area
  • Frozen Mango: If fresh mangoes are expensive, buy 2-3 bags of frozen mango chunks and thaw for puree
  • Group By Category: Create a list by clicking "Add to Cart" while filtering by pantry, condiments, spices, etc.
  • Prime Eligible: Filter for Prime-eligible items for 2-day delivery
  • Subscribe & Save: Oils, vinegars, and spices often have 5-20% discounts with Subscribe & Save

💡 Cost-Saving Ideas

  • Buy Frozen Mango instead of fresh for year-round cooking ($3-5/lb vs $1.50-2/lb for whole mangoes)
  • Get a Spice Bulk Set instead of individual spices (saves 30-50%)
  • Buy oils and vinegars in larger sizes (51 oz olive oil is cheaper per oz than 25 oz)
  • Skip items you already have — Most kitchens have salt, pepper, garlic powder, oils
  • Buy Shrimp frozen and in bulk (5 lb bags are cheaper per pound)

📝 Important Notes

  • Amounts listed assume you want to make MOST recipes multiple times
  • If you only want to make a few dishes, you can scale down quantities by 50%
  • Fresh produce shelf life: Limes (2-3 weeks), Cilantro (1 week), Fresh ginger (3 weeks), Bell peppers (1-2 weeks)
  • Frozen mangoes last 6-12 months — great for making puree whenever you need it
  • Frozen shrimp can be thawed quickly and lasts 9-12 months frozen
  • Spices stay fresh for 1-2 years when stored in cool, dark places
