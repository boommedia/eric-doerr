## Tips for Working with Mangoes

### Selection & Storage
- Choose mangoes that yield slightly to pressure
- Ripen at room temperature in a paper bag (3-5 days)
- Store ripe mangoes in refrigerator for up to 5 days
- Freeze mango puree or chunks for later use

### Preparation
- Use a sharp knife to cut along the flat pit
- Score flesh in crosshatch pattern, push skin up to cube
- Or use a mango peeler for strips
- Can puree fresh for sauces and dressings
