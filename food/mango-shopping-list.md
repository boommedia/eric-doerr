# Complete Mango Recipes - Master Shopping List for Amazon

## Amazon Shopping Tips

1. **Spice Bundles** — Search "spice set" or "spice rack" to get multiple spices at a discount
2. **Bulk Produce** — Order produce through Amazon Fresh or Prime Now if available in your area
3. **Frozen Mango** — If fresh mangoes are expensive, buy 2-3 bags of frozen mango chunks and thaw for puree
4. **Group By Category** — Create a list by clicking "Add to Cart" while filtering by pantry, condiments, spices, etc.
5. **Prime Eligible** — Filter for Prime-eligible items for 2-day delivery
6. **Subscribe & Save** — Oils, vinegars, and spices often have 5-20% discounts with Subscribe & Save

---

## Cost-Saving Ideas

- Buy **Frozen Mango** instead of fresh for year-round cooking (often $3-5/lb vs $1.50-2/lb for whole mangoes)
- Get a **Spice Bulk Set** instead of buying individual spices (saves 30-50%)
- Buy oils and vinegars in **larger sizes** (51 oz olive oil is cheaper per oz than 25 oz)
- **Skip items you already have** — Most kitchens have salt, pepper, garlic powder, oils
- Buy **Shrimp frozen** and in bulk (5 lb bags are cheaper per pound)

## Notes

- **Amounts listed assume you want to make MOST recipes multiple times**
- If you only want to make a few dishes, you can scale down quantities by 50%
- **Fresh produce shelf life:** Limes (2-3 weeks), Cilantro (1 week), Fresh ginger (3 weeks), Bell peppers (1-2 weeks)
- **Frozen mangoes last 6-12 months** — great for making puree whenever you need it
- **Frozen shrimp can be thawed quickly** and lasts 9-12 months frozen
- **Spices stay fresh for 1-2 years** when stored in cool, dark places
